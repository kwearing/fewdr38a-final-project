Join Aspendale Life Saving Club

Vision

To continuously provide a place welcoming for families and the community to feel safe, be educated, and enjoy the beach.

Mission

Supporting Aspendale beach goers through ongoing beach patrols, educating volunteers and the community in safe beach management and first-aid, in an atmosphere that is informative, fun, and supportive.
