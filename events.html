Annual event

Club 2 Club Open Water Swim Classic

Held in February on Port Phillip Bay in Melbourne's South East, Club2Club swim is one of Australia's premier open water swims

Organisers

The event is conducted by Aspendale and Edithvale Life Saving Clubs.

The Course

Open Water Swim Classic is 1.8km long and commences at 9:30am.

Starts: Edithvale Life Saving Club | The Esplanade, Edithvale

Ends: Aspendale Life Saving Club | Groves St, Aspendale

Cost

Pre-registration is $45 or $50 on the day.
