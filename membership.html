Membership Categories and Fees

Thank you for wanting to join our club, we can't wait to have you on board, at the club and in the water.

Membership type Annual fee Requirements Notes
Family $200 Includes up to 2 adult parent/guardians and dependent minors aged 5 to 17 as at 30 September. Children aged 5 to 17 as at 30 September must be part of a family membership with at least one parent/guardian over 18 years old. WWCC is compulsory for parents/guardians. Children under 5 cannot be registered. The membership fee does not include the fee for Nippers. This is an additional cost detailed below.
Active Family $150 Active membership includes if you patrol, are an age manager, or active in volunteering in the club. All members aged 5-13 years (Nippers) and members aged 14 -17 years (YIPS) who are not patrolling must be registered under a family membership with their parents or/guardians.
Individual Senior $90 Must be 18yrs and over as of 30 September Members 18+ who are not a legal guardian or parent of a Junior member must hold a patrol award and to complete the minimum patrolling hours as set by the committee.
Individual Youth $50 18-22 yrs and studying full time as of 30 September Youth member of the YIPS program (14yrs - 17yrs). Note: All members aged 14 -17 years who are not patrolling (Senior) must be registered under a family membership with their parents or/guardians.
Associate/Social members Single: $190; Family: $250 18 yrs and over Must provide a WWCC This membership allows for only individuals 18 years and over who are not intending to compete, patrol or train but would like to be a part of the social area of the club.

Additional Fees

Type Fee (per child/entry) Notes
Nippers $40 Each nipper requires a vest ($25) and cap ($15) which can be purchased on the day.
Training $20 Training fee is only payable if your child attends the weekday training program in addition to Sunday Nippers.
Standard carnival $35 Carnivals are charged separately to membership fees and is set by Life Saving Victoria for the cost of running each carnival. You also must be a financial member of Aspendale LSC to be able to register for a carnival.
State carnival $65 Carnivals are charged separately to membership fees and is set by Life Saving Victoria for the cost of running each carnival. You also must be a financial member of Aspendale LSC to be able to register for a carnival.
